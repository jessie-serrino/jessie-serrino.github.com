Biography

Jessie Serrino stays with her foster aunt and uncle on a farm on Tatooine. She is desperate to get off this planet and get to the Academy like her friends, but her uncle needs her for the next harvest. Meanwhile, an evil emperor has taken over the galaxy, and has constructed a formidable "Death Star" capable of destroying whole planets. Princess Leia, a leader in the resistance movement, acquires plans of the Death Star, places them in R2-D2, a droid, and sends him off to find Obi-Wan Kenobi. Before he finds him, R2-D2 ends up on Serrino's farm with her friend C-3PO. R2-D2 then wanders into the desert, and when Jessie follows, they eventually come across Obi-Wan. Will Jessie, Obi-Wan, and the two droids be able to destroy the Death Star, or will the Emperor rule forever?
